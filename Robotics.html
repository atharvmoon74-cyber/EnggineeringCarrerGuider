🤖 Robotics Engineering

Designing Intelligent Machines & Autonomous Systems

🌍 Overview

Robotics Engineering combines mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science to design intelligent machines.

Robotics engineers build robots for industries, healthcare, space exploration, military systems, and autonomous vehicles.

  • 🤖 Robot Design
  • 💻 Programming (Python / C++)
  • ⚙ Control Systems
  • 📡 Embedded Systems
  • 🧠 Artificial Intelligence
  • 📊 Computer Vision
