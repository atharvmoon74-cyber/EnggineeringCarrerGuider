🚀 Aerospace Engineering

Design the Future of Aviation, Space Exploration, and Rocket Science

Overview

Aerospace Engineering focuses on the design, development, testing, and production of aircraft, spacecraft, satellites, and missiles. It is divided into two main fields:

  • Aeronautical Engineering – Aircraft inside Earth atmosphere
  • Astronautical Engineering – Spacecraft and rockets

Skills Required

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Structural Analysis
  • Control Systems
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Programming (MATLAB / Python)
  • Problem Solving

Core Subjects
