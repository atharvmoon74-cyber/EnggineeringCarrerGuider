📡 Electronics & Communication Engineering

Designing Circuits, Chips, and Communication Technologies

🌍 Overview

Electronics and Communication Engineering focuses on electronic circuits, communication systems, semiconductors, and signal processing technologies.

ECE engineers design smartphones, satellites, IoT devices, wireless networks, and modern communication systems.
