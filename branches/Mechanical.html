⚙ Mechanical Engineering

Designing Machines, Engines, and Industrial Systems

🌍 Overview

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and broadest engineering branches focused on machines, engines, robotics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing systems.

Mechanical engineers design automobiles, aircraft engines, industrial machines, HVAC systems, and robotics technologies.
