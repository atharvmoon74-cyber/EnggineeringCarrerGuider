⚡ Electrical Engineering

Powering the Modern World with Electricity & Energy Systems

🌍 Overview

Electrical Engineering focuses on electricity, electronics, power systems, and electromagnetic technologies.

Electrical engineers design power grids, renewable energy systems, electric vehicles, and smart energy technologies.
