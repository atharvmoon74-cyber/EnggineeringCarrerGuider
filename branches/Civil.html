🏗 Civil Engineering

Designing Infrastructure that Builds the World

🌍 Overview

Civil Engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, bridges, dams, roads, and water supply systems.

Civil engineers play a key role in urban development, transportation systems, and sustainable infrastructure.
