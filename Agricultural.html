🌾 Agricultural Engineering

Engineering Solutions for Modern Farming & Food Production

🌍 Overview

Agricultural Engineering combines engineering principles with agriculture to improve farming efficiency, irrigation systems, crop production, and food processing technologies.

Agricultural engineers design machines, irrigation systems, greenhouses, and sustainable farming technologies.
